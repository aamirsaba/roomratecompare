Affiliate Disclosure

📅 Last Updated: June 1, 2026

📢 Transparency Commitment: RoomRateCompare believes in full transparency. We want you to know that we may earn commissions when you book through links on our site.

1. What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track referrals. When you click an affiliate link on our website and make a purchase or booking, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

2. Our Affiliate Partners

We participate in affiliate programs with the following partners:

  • Booking.com - Hotel and accommodation bookings
  • Agoda - Hotel and resort bookings
  • Expedia - Hotels, flights, and packages
  • Hotels.com - Hotel bookings
  • Travelpayouts - Various travel services
  • Klook - Tours and activities
  • GetYourGuide - Tours and experiences
  • Kiwitaxi - Airport transfers
  • Airalo - eSIM and connectivity
  • And other partners listed on our Partners page

3. How Affiliate Commissions Work

  • When you click an affiliate link, a cookie is placed in your browser
  • If you complete a booking within the cookie period (typically 30-90 days), we earn a commission
  • The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly
  • Affiliate commissions help us keep our services free for users

4. Affiliate Cookie Durations

  • Booking.com: Session-based
  • Agoda: 1 day
  • Expedia: 7 days
  • Klook: 7-30 days
  • Travelpayouts partners: 30-90 days

5. Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our content. We recommend hotels, travel services, and partners based on quality and value, not commission rates. We only partner with brands we trust.

6. No Extra Cost to You

Important: Using our affiliate links never increases the price you pay. In some cases, we may even have exclusive deals or discounts.

7. Sponsored Content

Some content may be sponsored. Sponsored posts will be clearly marked as such. All opinions remain our own.

8. Amazon Associates (if applicable)
