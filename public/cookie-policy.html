Cookie Policy

📅 Last Updated: June 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how RoomRateCompare uses cookies and similar tracking technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, and provide analytics.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Name Purpose Duration
Essential session_id Maintains your login session Session
Essential csrf_token Security protection Session
Preferences currency Remembers your currency preference 30 days
Analytics _ga Google Analytics - unique visitor tracking 2 years
Analytics _gid Google Analytics - user session tracking 24 hours
Analytics _gat Google Analytics - request rate limiting 1 minute
Affiliate travelpayouts_* Travelpayouts affiliate tracking 30-90 days
Affiliate booking_com_* Booking.com affiliate tracking Session

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Travelpayouts: Affiliate commission tracking
  • Booking.com, Agoda, Expedia: Affiliate partner tracking

4. Purpose of Cookies

  • Essential: Required for website functionality
  • Preferences: Remember your settings (currency, language)
  • Analytics: Help us improve our website
  • Marketing/Affiliate: Track affiliate referrals and commissions

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner. By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Do Not Track

Our website responds to Do Not Track (DNT) signals. You can enable DNT in your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

For questions about our Cookie Policy, contact privacy@roomratecompare.com
